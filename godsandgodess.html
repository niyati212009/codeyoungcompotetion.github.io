What are gods?

Gods are the supreme, universal soul, Braham is the god, he is the universe and everything in it. He has no form and no limits. We have up to 330 million gods but Braham is the ultimate god. All those 330 Million gods are different forms and when combined it creates Braham.

Braham

Braham is the "creater" and is the first member to the hindu trinity. He is the creator of the universe.

Shiva

Shiva is the "destroyer" and is the third member to the hundu trinity. He destroys the universe to get ready for each cycle of time.

Vishnu

Vishnu is the "preserver" and second member of the hindu trinity. He maintains the universe's order and harmony which is created by braham than destroyed by shiva for there next creation. Vishnu has many forms and several avatars that are still worshipped today

Vishnu's avatars

Khrishna is one of Vishnu's avatars, he is full of pranks and is known for his promise made to humanity that he will manifest himself and descend to earth whenever dharma declines has sustained Hindu belief in the Supreme Being over thousands of years.

Ganesh

Ganesh is "the remover of obsticals", he is the first son of Shiva and is seen with a elephant head. Ganesh helps you with whatever problem you are facing
